Online, I go by the moniker of Gaiacrusher9, or Gaia for short, but IRL, I’m Autumn, a 26 year-old nerd, currently pursuing a Bachelor’s in Information Technology, but playing a bunch of games in the meanwhile. I started my Youtube channel back on May 16th, 2009, a fresh-faced and very shy boy who had just started a degree in Bio-Medical Sciences, and was encouraged by my friends (and fellow Youtube content creators) to try my hand at the world of Let’s Plays.

“Wait though”, you ask, “A boy?” Beyond the adorable mascot that I’ve used since the inception of my channel (something that came of a habit of drawing cat faces, and discovering a certain item in Gaia Online), one of my most distinguishing features is that I am a Male-to-Female Transsexual! I began seeking counseling back in 2011, began medically transitioning a few months after, and completed my Sexual Reassignment Surgery back in March of 2013. Because of these experiences, I even made a short series on Youtube detailing some information about the whole transitioning process! If you ever have any questions, feel free to ask me, I love talking about it all. I’m also a Type 1 Diabetic too, so if you ever need someone to talk to about that as well, I’m more than happy to help :D

My Youtube channel primarily deals with Let’s Play Playthroughs of various video games from across all kinds of platforms and generations. From LEGO games, to RPGs, to even the more odd-ball games like Chibi-Robo, I strive to create quality content (if maybe abit mature at certain times) while simply trying to be myself and have a good time with both the games and my audience at large. I also occasionally branch out to make more silly content videos, and have been working to incorporate more things into my channel, such as content that I run in multiplayer games such as Final Fantasy XIV (Autumn Dawn on Ultros, if you’re ever curious enough), and hopefully even streaming on Twitch in the future!
